Our Services
Service

Southern Utah Pools tailors to your needs. Being in the pool servicing industry for the past 14 years has given me instinctive sense for the obvious problems arising in equipment failure. I can easily assess and troubleshoot all issues in pool equipment.

Equipment and Repair

I repair all heaters, circulation pumps, water feature pumps, jet pumps, lights, automatic covers, plumbing, and new equipment installs.

Water Chemistry

The most effective way I have found to treat the pool is through salt chlorine generators and using chlorine and bromine feeders.

Pool Sweeps/Cleaner
